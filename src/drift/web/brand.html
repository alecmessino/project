01

The mark

Aligned confluence · frozen

Five tributaries, Investments, Taxes, Estate, Liquidity, and the Relationships that coordinate them, enter from one shared origin and step through a true 45° convergence into a single, heavier trunk: what you keep. The outflow is held at length and cut square, so the mark reads as coordination and structure, not direction or speed. Straight geometry, square caps, no curves.

Five streams, always. The mark holds all five at every size. It is never redrawn with fewer lines and never swapped for a simpler glyph, down to 16px. One silhouette earns recognition; two would spend it.

03
Colour
blue names the brand · teal is data

    
Editorial blue #2C5878 is the mark's only identity colour, and carries wayfinding: the mark, section eyebrows, the running-head marker, active nav. Bright teal #15806A is reserved for data only, positive values, links, the recompute pulse. Teal never touches the mark and never carries identity.

04
Typography

    
Wordmark and descriptor are set in Satoshi (--sans), tracked caps, the wordmark at --logo-word-spacing .15em, the descriptor wider. The Signature descriptor is the one place the editorial serif (Erode) appears, quiet and lowercase-titled, so it reads as a colophon rather than a logo.

  


  

    
05
Usage

    

      
Do
    
        
  • Keep all five streams at every size, down to 16px.
    • 
        
  • Keep the mark's height of clear-space on every side.
    • 
        
  • Editorial blue on limestone; white or limestone on navy.
    • 
        
  • Favicon, editorial-blue tile; avatar, editorial-blue disc.
    • 
        
  • Use the heavier favicon cut at ≤ 32px.
    • 
      

      
Don't
    
        
  • Reduce the stream count, no three-stream or simplified cut.
    • 
        
  • Colour the mark anything but editorial blue; never use teal for identity.
    • 
        
  • Two-tone the wordmark, or set it anything but tracked caps.
    • 
        
  • Curve the streams, shorten the trunk, or round the terminal.
    • 
        
  • Add a box, shadow, gradient, or rounded corner to the mark.
