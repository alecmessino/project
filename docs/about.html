Our story

Why we built Driftwood.

The world's largest investors have spent decades refining how portfolios are built. Individual investors deserve the same level of thinking. That idea became Driftwood.

Why Driftwood exists

I spent six years at Dimensional Fund Advisors, helping some of the country's largest advisory firms build institutional investment portfolios.

One project focused on something most investors never see. We found portfolios quietly losing wealth to tax-inefficient funds. Once advisors saw the numbers, they moved more than $400 million.

Then something stopped making sense.

Institutions received this level of attention every day. Families rarely did.

The tools already existed. The discipline already existed. The architecture already existed.

It simply wasn't reaching the people who needed it most.

Driftwood was built to change that.

What we believe

The goal isn't the most exciting portfolio. It's one you're glad to own in twenty years.

How that belief becomes a portfolio is set out in How We Invest. The longer worldview behind it lives in The Driftwood Principles.
