The Coordination Review
A defined engagement

Most financial mistakes are not investment mistakes.

They are coordination mistakes. Your CPA, your attorney, your insurance agent, and your advisor are each good at their own work, and almost no one is accountable for how the pieces fit together. The Coordination Review is where that gap gets found, measured, and assigned an owner.

A cancer diagnosis is not treated by one brilliant doctor. It is decided by a room, and one person stays accountable for the whole patient. That is the attending physician.

Wealth borrowed the specialists and forgot the attending. Driftwood is the attending: we charge to coordinate decisions, not to manage a number.
