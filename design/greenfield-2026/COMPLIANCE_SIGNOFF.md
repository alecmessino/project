## C. Three things I recommend you strike or change

1. **A5 (~$30,000/yr).** Cutting the dollar translation removes the only figure on the site that
   could read as a promised return. Cite Vanguard's 3% and stop there.

   a reproducible assumption set is the one an examiner will ask about first.

   but arguable. A lower rate weakens the exhibit slightly and strengthens the disclosure posture.
