Households and business owners navigating real complexity, especially around a liquidity event: a business sale, an option exercise, a concentrated position, an inheritance, or a move across state lines.

Coordination across investments, taxes, estate, and cash flow, so decisions reinforce one another instead of quietly working against one another. What a family office does, for individual households.
