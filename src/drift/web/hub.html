Driftwood

Cross-sectional & time-series momentum research — region & factor equity ETFs.

Driftwood is a quantitative wealth-architecture platform — a systematic global-equity engine wrapped in a holistic, after-tax asset-location framework. It measures how persistently an instrument has drifted relative to its own volatility, then coordinates account structure (taxable / Traditional / Roth), state and federal tax, estate step-up, and tax-loss harvesting into one after-tax plan. Research and illustrative modeling; it flags and sizes trends, it does not place trades.
